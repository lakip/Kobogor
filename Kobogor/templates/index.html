About Us

It's a women organisation that provides holistic support to women through social and economic empowerment programmes to enable them to thrive and fully contribute,participate in the community

Kobogor women group is a social economic empowerment entity that works to help the community co-created sustainable solutions to build and better themselves their families and community. The group was founded by two women Mrs Bornice Mutai and Gladys Chumek and as incrementally grown to now over 250 members. The group operates through tailored programmes to meet the special needs of the community. It was founded in 2019 June and currently operates within kipkelion east constituency of kericho county kenya.
